The Signature at The Victory Residences

The Signature at The Victory Residences is a blend of colonial modern-style architecture and authentic Thaiesthetics, located only 2 minutes walk from Victory Monument Skytrain Station and only 10 minutes walk from Phaya Thai Airport Link Station. It features a state-of-the-art outdoor pool and well-equipped gym and spa. Free WiFi is available in all areas. Guest rooms are elegantly furnished with Thai décor accents and Art European-style wood. They all include a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a seating area and private bathroom.
